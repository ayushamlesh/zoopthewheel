Tata Tigor              PRICE RANGE ₹ 6.57 Lakh - ₹ 9.03 Lakh

Variants: The Tata Tigor is available in four variants including XE, XM, XZ, and XZ+. Tata Tigor price starts at ₹ 6.57 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 9.03 Lakh. Part of Tata’s model offensive, the updated Tigor gets new Impact 2.0 design philosophy. It also gets a new paint scheme, updated cabin and some new features including digital instrument cluster. So the Tigor continues to be the homegrown compact sedan which undercuts its rivals by a considerable margin.

Hyundai Verna              PRICE RANGE ₹ 10.77 Lakh - ₹ 18.15 Lakh

The Verna facelift may not pull your heartstrings with its design, but it sure is a good package. Perhaps, it is one of the better built sedans in its segment, gets a number of segment-first features and there are enough and more engine/transmission options one can choose from. Sure, the rear seat space is just about adequate, but with the value it offers with its price-to-features ratio, it is hard to not consider.

Hyundai Aura            PRICE RANGE ₹ 6.95 Lakh - ₹ 10.86 Lakh

Hyundai has reinstated the compact sedan segment contest with the introduction of the all- new Aura. Based on the Grand i10 Nios, the Aura has a radical design on the outside combined with a feature-loaded cabin. It’s powered by Hyundai’s proven engine options with a choice of CNG fuel as well. Except for the debateable design, what’s not to like in the Aura.

Maruti Suzuki Ciaz               PRICE RANGE ₹ 9.90 Lakh - ₹ 13.41 Lakh

If driving fun or a fast yet efficient automatic gearbox tech are your priorities, you would be better off with rivals, but if you are looking for a comfortable city runabout that’s big on space and great value, the Ciaz is hard to fault.
