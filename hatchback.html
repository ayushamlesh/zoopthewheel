TATA TIAGO              PRICE RANGE ₹4.99 - 7.09 Lakh*

With the Impact 2.0 design language, the updated Tata Tiago not only looks much more appealing, it also appears more mature than before. The sweeping headlamps and sharp grille add to this updated design but the rear remains more or less unchanged. However, the updated cabin gets newer features to keep the Tiago relevant for entry-level hatchback buyers.

TATA Altroz              PRICE RANGE Rs. 5.84 - 9.59 Lakh

The Tata Altroz has 1 Diesel Engine and 1 Petrol Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 1497 cc while the Petrol engine is 1199 cc . It is available with Manual transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Altroz has a mileage of 18.13 to 25.11 kmpl & Ground clearance of Altroz is 165mm. The Altroz is a 5 seater 4 cylinder car and has length of 3990mm, width of 1755mm and a wheelbase of 2501mm.

Hyundai Grand i10 Nios            PRICE RANGE ₹ 6.15 Lakh - ₹ 9.88 Lakh

price starts at ₹ 6.15 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 9.88 Lakh. The price of Petrol version for Grand i10 Nios ranges between ₹ 6.15 Lakh - ₹ 9.10 Lakh, the price of CNG version for Grand i10 Nios ranges between ₹ 8.07 Lakh - ₹ 8.68 Lakh and the price of Diesel version for Grand i10 Nios ranges between ₹ 8.31 Lakh - ₹ 9.88 Lakh. The Grand i10 Nios, with its striking design, is now an able competitor against the Maruti Suzuki Swift in terms of looks. Sure, it isn’t exactly fun to drive and has a busy ride. But, it comes with premium interiors, is loaded with features and offers the convenience of an automatic (AMT) even with the diesel engine and an option of CNG as well. And if Hyundai’s service is anything to go by, it will be a fuss-free hatchback to own.

Hyundai i20              PRICE RANGE ₹ 7.98 Lakh - ₹ 13.24 Lakh

Hyundai i20 price starts at ₹ 7.98 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 13.24 Lakh. The price of Petrol version for i20 ranges between ₹ 7.98 Lakh - ₹ 13.24 Lakh and the price of Diesel version for i20 ranges between ₹ 9.56 Lakh - ₹ 12.58 Lakh. The new-gen Hyundai i20 drops the ‘Elite’ from its name and takes the game forward with its striking looks and many first-in-class features. As before, it is spacious on the inside, but we expected slightly improved material quality especially since everything else has moved up a notch. What’s more, with no less than five powertrain choices, there’s an i20 for everyone. Overall, the new i20 remains a proper family hatch with added razzle-dazzle.

Maruti Suzuki Ignis              PRICE RANGE ₹ 5.76 Lakh - ₹ 8.64 Lakh.

With the new and updated Ignis, Maruti Suzuki wants to position it as a compact SUV. What it actually is, is a practical little hatchback that offers excellent handling, usability and is backed by Maruti's vast service network. Sure, the quirky design may not be to everyone’s liking, but it is a unique alternative to the Hyundai Grand i10 Nios and Maruti Suzuki Swift. Maruti Suzuki Ignis price starts at ₹ 5.76 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 8.64 Lakh.

Maruti Suzuki Baleno              PRICE RANGE ₹ 6.84 - ₹ 10.56 Lakh

Maruti Suzuki Baleno price starts at ₹ 6.84 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 10.56 Lakh. Yet another winner from Maruti and deservedly so. It has good performance, drives well, cabin has ample space and is feature-packed. The cherry on the cake is the pricing and wide service backing from Maruti's Nexa dealerships. Overall, the Baleno is the most affordable and sensible premium hatchback you can buy today.

Maruti Suzuki Swift              PRICE RANGE ₹ 6.78 Lakh - ₹ 10.07 Lakh.

Maruti Suzuki Swift price starts at ₹ 6.78 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 10.07 Lakh. The price of Petrol version for Swift ranges between ₹ 6.78 Lakh - ₹ 10.07 Lakh. With the new facelifted Swift, Maruti Suzuki has equipped it with more features and a new K12 series Dual Jet Dual VVT engine with idle start-stop function. The Swift continues with a stylish stance, a spacious cabin that’s now more feature-laden, while also promising a great driving pleasure. With the choice of both manual and AMT gearboxes, the Swift continues its streak of being as easy to recommend as earlier.
