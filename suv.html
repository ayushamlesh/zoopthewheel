TATA Punch              PRICE RANGE ₹ 6.38 Lakh - ₹ 10.89 Lakh

Tata Punch price starts at ₹ 6.38 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 10.89 Lakh. The new Tata Punch is a genuinely impressive compact vehicle, one that’s big on style, ride comfort, features and practicality.

Tata Harrier            PRICE RANGE ₹ 16.99 Lakh - ₹ 25.12 Lakh

Variants The variants of the Tata Harrier SUV are broadly divided into six variants - XE, XM, XT, XT+, XZ, and XZ+.. The Tata Harrier is certainly a looker. That, combined with the Land Rover-derived underpinnings, spacious interiors and a long equipment-list, makes for a potent package overall. However, the engine is unrefined and cabin insulation needs work too. Even after the BS6 update, It doesn’t get a petrol engine option or an AWD. But it does get some features which it missed out before, like a panoramic sunroof, powered seats and an auto-dimming IRVM.

Tata Safari              PRICE RANGE ₹ 17.67 Lakh - ₹ 27.52 Lakh.

Agreed that without a rugged, body-on-ladder construction, or any sort of four-wheel-drive option, and being a front-wheel-drive only doesn’t give the new SUV enough cred to carry the Tata Safari torch. But, as a six or seven-seater SUV for the urban world, this new Safari has almost every box checked! It’s usable, comfortable, drivable, feature-rich, and it has a street presence.

Hyundai Creta              PRICE RANGE ₹ 11.89 - ₹ 21.12 Lakh

Hyundai launched the new-gen Creta in the Indian market aiming to gain back its segment- leader position which was lost to the Kia Seltos. Available across 14 trims with three engine and gearbox options, the Creta continues to be one of the most feature-laden SUV. Although the new design might take some time getting used to, the strong brand-following the Creta enjoys in India should continue with the new model.

Hundai Venue              PRICE RANGE ₹ 8.07 Lakh - ₹ 13.77 Lakh.

Hyundai entered the sub-four metre SUV segment with the debut of the Venue back in May 2019. The model is currently available with three engine options across seven variants that include E, S, S(O), S+, SX, EX(O) Exe, and SX (O). The Venue is yet another example of Hyundai hitting all the right notes. It nails the brief about being a compact family SUV. It is powered by refined engine options and has a comfortable cabin that’s well stocked with features. And with its well-sorted dynamics, the Venue is our pick among the crossovers.
